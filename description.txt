Displays an item's rarity's color around the item (lining the item slot) as a border. This mod is directly inspired by "Rarity Borders" with what is, in my eyes, much better execution.

Natively works with Magic Storage, Boss Checklist, and any other mod that uses item slots!

Want to design your own border and have it added to the mod? Join the Discord through our mod homepage.
The Discord is a great place to post suggestions and report bugs too. Config suggestions are especially encouraged!
